## Summary

Tussel covers the core subset well: basic patterns, common transforms, standard effects, and fundamental combinators. The gaps are primarily in advanced pattern algebra (polymetric, chunk, hurry) and some effects (vowel, shape). The Tidal parser in Tussel covers ~60 of 100+ Tidal methods.
