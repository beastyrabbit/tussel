## What is Tussel?

Tussel is a **terminal-first livecoding daemon** for pattern-based music. It takes the musical ideas from [Tidal Cycles](https://tidalcycles.org/) and [Strudel](https://strudel.cc/), wraps them in typed TypeScript, and runs entirely on your local machine — no browser required.

Write patterns. Hear them instantly. Edit live.

## Why Tussel?

- **TypeScript-native** — full type safety, editor autocomplete, familiar tooling
- **Terminal daemon** — no browser, no GUI overhead; runs in your terminal with live reload
- **Three formats** — `script-ts` (livecoding), `scene-ts` (typed modules), `scene-json` (tooling)
- **Strudel/Tidal compatible** — growing parity with existing livecoding ecosystems
- **Local-first** — your samples, your machine, offline by default

## Project Status

Tussel is **experimental and under active development**. The core pattern engine, DSL, and audio rendering work well for the supported subset of features. Full Strudel/Tidal parity is an ongoing effort.

See the [feature comparison](/tussel/compare/feature-matrix/) for current coverage.
