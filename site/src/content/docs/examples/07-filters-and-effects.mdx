Effects transform sounds after synthesis. Low-pass filters remove high frequencies, delay creates echoes, and room reverb adds space.

## Code

## What's Happening

- `.room(0.3)` adds 30% reverb, giving the drums a sense of space
- `.delay(0.25)` adds a quarter-cycle delay echo
- `.lpf(600)` is a low-pass filter at 600Hz — the saw wave sounds muffled and warm
- `sd:1` and `sd:2` select different snare variations from the sample bank
- `.pan(0.5)` moves hi-hats to the right stereo channel
- Effects chain from left to right: the order you write them is the order they apply
